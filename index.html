The focus of this research is to forecast Walmart's weekly sales. Additionally, a statistical analysis of the weekly sales of 45 Walmart stores is carried out. This study uses Time Series and Machine Learning modals such KNN, Random Forest, and Linear Regression. To identify which modal is most efficient, these modals are evaluated. The highest accuracy, 97.896%, is provided by Random Forest.
